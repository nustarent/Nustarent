// WHO WE ARE

About

Photos by Rayden ✦ The Goat

NUSTAR was not the plan. But it was always going to happen.

It started freshman year of high school. 2019. Five kids found out there was a music studio tucked away in a room after school and thought why not. They called themselves Dentes Country. It was fun. It was chaotic. And honestly they kept fooling around and being lazy, so it never went anywhere.

Nate and Wyolkens linked up that same year. Two people who just clicked when it came to music. They wanted to cook together from day one.

But Nate did not stop after Dentes Country fell apart. He tried again with a new group called Troubled Youth. Did not go anywhere either. Then came Origins. Different lineup, same dream. Then COVID hit and shut everything down before it could breathe.

Years passed. Around 2023, Wyolkens was on a college campus and found a music studio. He immediately thought of Nate. Not because it was the logical choice. Just because when he thought about making music, Nate was the person he wanted to be in the room with. Even though Nate had moved away during sophomore year they never lost touch. Wyolkens made the call, Nate was in, and they locked in.

They had no real plan. No engineer. No idea how to record properly. They booked studio sessions anyway and figured they would figure it out. They did not figure it out. Nobody helped them. Nothing got done. But the idea never died.

In 2024, Nate stepped out on his own under the name WACKO PROPHET and released an instrumental album called THE PARTY ENDED AN HOUR AGO. No lyrics, just beats. If they had figured out that college studio maybe it would have been different. But that album exists and it started something.

Then 2025 came. Six years after that first after school session in 2019, Nate still had the vision he always had. Wyolkens said forget trying to do it alone. They found a studio through a random search, locked in with the right people, and booked their sessions for real. The studio was called Dumblit.

The album process was not easy. Getting the right people together took real effort. Wyolkens reached out to everyone he knew looking for female vocalists. Nate did the same. Three women came through. One said she was in and then ghosted Nate completely. You cannot make that up. But the ones who showed up delivered, and the album got made.

The first single New Egypt dropped October 3rd, 2025. The album LAFAYETTE followed on October 24th, 2025.

Next up is UNISEX, dropping June 2026. That is where NUSTAR truly began. And it is only getting started.
